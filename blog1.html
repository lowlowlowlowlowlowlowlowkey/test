My Vacation

We went to our province, Bacolod, to celebrate Christmas together with our family.
But unfortunately, Typhoon Odette made it hard to enjoy but we managed to
spend every minute of our time with our loved ones. My dad enjoyed it because
he got reunited with his best friend which he didn't saw for 15 years and I am happy for him.
We went to different places in Bacolod and the one I enjoyed the most
is the Seaside Restaurant because the ambiance of the place was nice.
Me and my cousins got reunited after 7 years and we spent every time of the day
talking and bonding with each other. My mom also enjoyed it
because we spent New Year's Eve with her family and her favorite brother.
